# Payment Method Logos

Place your payment method logo images in this directory.

## Required Images:

- `visa.svg` or `visa.png` - Visa logo
- `mastercard.svg` or `mastercard.png` - Mastercard logo
- `jcb.svg` or `jcb.png` - JCB logo
- `amex.svg` or `amex.png` - American Express logo
- `dinersclub.svg` or `dinersclub.png` - Diners Club logo
- `discover.svg` or `discover.png` - Discover logo
- `paypal.svg` or `paypal.png` - PayPal logo
- `paidy.svg` or `paidy.png` - Paidy logo
- `alipay.svg` or `alipay.png` - Alipay+ logo
- `unionpay.svg` or `unionpay.png` - UnionPay logo
- `atome.svg` or `atome.png` - Atome logo

## Image Specifications:

- **Format**: SVG preferred (for scalability), PNG acceptable
- **Size**: Approximately 50px width, 20px height
- **Background**: Transparent preferred
- **Quality**: High resolution for PNG (2x for retina displays)

## Fallback:

If an image is not found, the component will automatically display the payment method name as text instead.
